# Zynkr Business Strategy

## Vision

Empowering everyone to seamlessly collaborate with AI through AI consulting, unleashing their productivity and helping them refocus on humanity's most important and valuable activities.

## Mission

Helping professionals enhance their workplace competitiveness in the AI era, from individual growth to enterprise transformation.

---

## Brand

**Zynkr** — inspired by "synchronization & thinking," symbolizing how people and AI can work in harmony to amplify productivity, creativity, and impact.

### Brand Story

Zynkr was born from a belief that AI should not replace people, but empower them. In a world of increasing automation, the real challenge is not whether machines will take over, but whether humans can evolve fast enough to harness them. We exist to make AI approachable, actionable, and personal — helping individuals and organizations synchronize with AI, unlocking their productivity while enabling them to refocus on what truly matters: creativity, strategy, and human connection.

### What We Do

We provide AI consulting, training, and enablement solutions for both individuals and enterprises. Our approach blends education, advisory services, and technology to help people adapt, compete, and thrive in the AI era.

### The Three Gaps We Close

| Gap | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Knowledge Gap** | We help others find the answer to the problem |
| **Cognitive Gap** | We help others understand how to solve the problem |
| **Execution Gap** | We help others learn how to use the answers by doing |

### We Sell

- Practical, human-centered AI enablement
- Tools and training that enhance competitiveness in the AI economy
- Modular AI agents that turn expertise into scalable, repeatable solutions
- A pathway from personal productivity → team efficiency → enterprise transformation

### We Do NOT Sell

- AI hype or fearmongering
- One-size-fits-all solutions
- Standalone tech without guidance
- Consultancy that ends with a PowerPoint but no real-world impact

## Strategy Blueprint

```
More Users → More Data & Cases → Stronger Agents → More Experts Onboard → Richer Ecosystem → More Users
```

## Strategic Focus & Growth Opportunities

### Advisory Services: Opportunities and Constraints

- **High Value, Low Scalability:** Deep, hands-on consulting (IBM-style) creates significant impact but is difficult to scale.
- **Strategic Recommendation:** In the early stage, leverage senior instructors to deliver deep engagement projects → build case studies → convert into modular products. Over time, transition toward "consulting toolkits + training products" to enable systemization and scalability.

### Dual Track Approach: Courses + Consulting

#### B2C — Modular Products
- Course pricing, tiered packaging, funnel design, and personalized engagement
- Core priority: Build an evergreen sales system (automation, content reuse, continuous funnel)

#### B2B — Modularized Consulting Solutions
- Enterprise training + AI workshops + workflow optimization toolkits
- Use a **pilot + retainer** model to deepen partnerships:
  - **Pilot:** Select a scope small enough to be feasible but still meaningful and valuable
  - **Retainer:** Monthly subscription covering consulting, operations, automation, optimization, analytics, and internal training

## Operations

### Operation Pillars

#### Brand & Marketing
- **Positioning:** Trusted AI enablement partner, emphasizing productivity, creativity, and human-centered adoption
- **Channels:** Content marketing, media buys, SEO, website, social media, community (Discord)
- **CRM Integration:** Link marketing funnels to CRM for better nurturing and forecasting
- **Thought Leadership:** Publish evergreen content (case studies, webinars, success stories)

#### Consultant & Sales
- **Sales Enablement:** Prospecting, discovery, pitching, objection handling, and account management
- **B2C Funnel:** Lead magnets → modular courses → coaching upsell; community for continuous LTV
- **B2B Model:** Pilot-to-retainer journey, starting with measurable ROI pilots → recurring consulting
- **Cross-sell:** From workshops to workflow consulting and agent solutions

#### Operations
- **Operating Rhythm:** Weekly reviews of priorities, blockers, and progress
- **Foundations:** Clear roles & responsibilities, centralized documentation, standardized processes
- **Automation:** Streamline CRM updates, reporting, and scheduling
- **Ops as Product:** Treat internal workflow refinement as a testbed for client-facing solutions

#### Learning (Design & Deliver)
- **Design:** Distill insights into teachable modules, select delivery formats (self-paced, live cohort, workshops), prepare materials/scripts
- **Delivery:** Upload courses to chosen platforms, ensure smooth learner onboarding, package signature frameworks into brand IP
- **Post-production:** High-quality recording, editing, and distribution

#### Development Operations
- **Process Excellence:** Shadowing, process mining, mapping, digitalization assessment (RPA, ROI, RICE), optimization, SIPOC, and workflow frameworks
- **Build Agents:** Architecture/design (SIPOC, swimlane, modular packaging), prompt engineering, agentic workflow (node design, chaining, testing)
- **Go-to-Market:** UAT, staged rollout, data analysis, onboarding, training, change management, marketplace management

#### Product & Tech
- Build the foundation for the AI Agent Platform — modular, reusable, and extensible
- Prioritize internal automation: scheduling, reporting, data flows
- Develop knowledge-into-agent pipelines from playbooks, consulting toolkits, and course materials
- Create early prototypes for marketplace-ready agents (Resume Optimizer, Process Diagnostic, Workflow Builder)

#### People & Talent
- Grow a bench of instructors and consultants: senior talent for credibility, junior associates for scalability
- Create internal upskilling programs for continuous adaptation
- Foster a culture of experimentation + reflection: weekly ops → learnings → playbooks
- Define career pathways blending consulting, teaching, and productization roles

#### Finance & Admin
- Ensure financial health, reporting, compliance, and scalable back-office systems
- Support growth with lean, reliable administrative processes

### Strategic Operations Layers

| Layer | Focus |
|---|---|
| **Knowledge Ops** | Funnel insights into a knowledge gap map; encode into reusable artifacts (playbooks, cases, templates); treat every engagement as a mini-lab |
| **Process Ops** | Translate knowledge gaps into structured thinking steps; reconstruct workflows into repeatable blueprints; ensure all frameworks are modular |
| **DevOps for Automation** | Move validated thinking steps into automated workflows (RPA pipelines, LLM agents); build internal automation first |
| **MLOps for Intelligence** | Operationalize a Knowledge Graph; apply RAGAS for accuracy and reliability; build monitoring loops from client workflows |

### Core Operations

#### Service Delivery & Fulfillment
- **Online courses:** Automated delivery of course links, materials, and Notion resources with pre-course guidance emails and completion reminders
- **Consulting & workshops:** Conducted via Google Meet, supported by consultant record templates
- **Automated purchase flow:** Payment → auto-trigger Gmail/ConvertKit for receipts and deliverables

#### Workflow & Project Management
- **Project calendar:** Cross-functional timelines for courses, marketing, and consulting projects
- **Project tracker:** Task breakdown, weekly Sprint Reviews and retrospectives
- **B2B projects:** Milestone-based tracking with regular progress/quality reviews

#### Process & SOP Design
- SOP Library covering course launches, event hosting, consulting delivery, and community engagement
- Each SOP includes visualized steps, examples, and checklists
- Maintained in Google Drive, supplemented with video tutorials

#### Customer Operations & Support
- FAQ hub and satisfaction surveys (30d, 60d, 90d checkpoints)
- Client Success Tracker: feedback, pain points, and upsell potential
- **Service SLA:** Responses within 48h weekdays, 72h weekends

#### Admin & Back Office
- Structured Google Drive indexing (courses / consulting / marketing / finance)
- Monthly invoicing, accounting, tax filing, and audit reviews
- Standardized contract templates for consultant fees, workshop agreements, and SOWs

## Consultancy Methodology

### Phase 1 — Business Discovery & Diagnosis
- Prospecting
- Discover – 5D Diagnosis
- Shadowing
- Process mapping (SIPOC)
- Digitalization assessment
- Decide what to automate (Subjectivity vs. Traffic, Impact vs. Effort, Risk Assessment)
- Redesigning the E2E experience and framing the architecture (FE/BE/DB)
- Process optimization (3×3×3×3 matrix)
- Visualizing the workflow; turning SIPOC into tech architecture

### Phase 2 — AI & Agentic Workflow Prototyping
- Mirror the process, slot in assistants & knowledge
- Mix of interactions and assistant node design
- Guiding principles for individual assistant nodes
- Consider types of routines
- Vibe prompting

### Phase 3 — Change Management & Adoption
- Governing knowledge at scale
- Finding the answer from the right place
- Configuration to surface answers at the right time
- Knowledge management and the operation flywheel

## Action Plan

### Phase 0 — Foundation & Kick-off _(4 weeks)_ ✅
- Finalize North Star & 3 Horizons
- Initial version of Operation Guide, OKR Board, SOP Hub
- Finance: Simple P&L projection, sign tax advisor
- Product: Select 1 core B2C course + 1 B2B MVP workshop, at least 3 SOPs ready
- Generate demand: Branding, content marketing, publishing pipeline
- Build marketplace: Training courses, POC solutions, AI agents
- Establish supply: Course design & delivery, consulting services, process mapping, building AI agents, go-to-market

### Phase 1 — Cash Cow Validation _(8–10 weeks)_
**Goal:** Build 1–2 cash cows and break even in 6 months

- **B2C:** Launch core course (Accupass / Course platform)
  - Evergreen mini-funnel: Lead magnet → Email → Sales page
  - Run paid/low-price "AI in 90 mins" pilots
- **Milestones:**
  - B2C: ≥ 100 course sales OR ≥ NT$150k revenue
  - B2B: ≥ 2 companies express contract intent

### Phase 2 — Systemize & Initial Scale _(12–16 weeks, Month 6–8)_
- Hire virtual assistants & fully ramp up
- Standardize SOPs → Training tutorials → Delegate to assistants
- Expand B2C product lines (1–2 courses/templates), upsell/cross-sell
- Package B2B consulting toolkit (diagnostic sheet, proposal deck, pricing sheet)
- KPI dashboard (revenue, AOV, lead → sale conversion)
- **Targets:**
  - Evergreen Funnel monthly revenue ≥ NT$200k for 2 consecutive months
  - B2B: ≥ 5 signed clients (including pilot upgrades)

### Phase 3 — Scale & Diversify _(20–24 weeks)_
- Transition founder role into Strategy + Sales focus
- Bring in 1–2 core partners, outsource long-term (ads, automation)
- Paid ads/SEO scaling with clear CAC & LTV, ROAS ≥ 2
- Launch community or membership model for predictable revenue
- **Targets:**
  - B2C: Monthly recurring revenue ≥ NT$300k
  - B2B: ≥ NT$3M in signed annual contracts or retainers
  - B2B: Annual retainer contracts & corporate training cohorts
  - Finance: Cash flow ≥ 6 months run rate, net margin target 40%
